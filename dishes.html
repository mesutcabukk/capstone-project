Some info text hereCarrot Salads With Yoghurt

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Garlic
  • 1 cup yoghurt
  • Dill for putting the edges

Recipe: Put the grated carrots in pot. Stir the carrots at medium-high heat. Turn the heat off when then carrots are tender. Wait till the carrots are cold. Grat the garlic and put some salt in it. Add the garlic and yoghurt in carrots. Stir them. Chop the dill for putting the edges on plate. Bon appetit.

Some info text hereCarrot Cake Balls
I am gonna give you easy cake Recipe for kids…….
Ingredient Carrot Cake Balls
  • 8 gratted carrots
  • 3 cups desiccated coconut
  • 3 packet biscuit with kakao
  • 1 cup water
Recipe: Place the gratted black carrots and 1 cup water into pot and place the pot cover on it and stay 10 minutes and mix the ingredients now and again till cooked. Use your hands to press and make the biscuit dust. Place into pot the biscuit dust and mix the ingredients. And again use your hands to press and shape the mixture into balls. Roll in desiccated coconut and its ready. I loved them. My all nephews loved them. I hope you enjoy them too. Eat and enjoy …. Bon appetit ….
Some info text hereSaj Tava
  • 500 gr lamb, cut in small pieces
  • Small pieces lamb tail fat or 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 medium size onion, chopped in small pieces
  • 1 big size tomatoe, peeled and chopped in small pieces
  • 2 banana peppers and red peppers, cut in small pieces
  • 3 pinch of isot pepper (The core rate of zero and a silky texture and grown only in the world isot Sanliurfa in Turkey is a type of pepper)
  • 2 pinch of red pepper
  • 1 tbsp red pepper paste
  • Salt
  • Savory
  • Garlic

Recipe:Let’s start to cook the lamb with butter. The cooking process is supposed to be in a saj tava (a Turkish cooking vessel) or If you don’t have it, you can use wok (a Chinese cooking vessel). Stir the lamb constantly. When the color turn to light brown, stop stirring. Continue to cook the lamb with its own juice. When there is no more juice in saj tava, add the rest of ingredients. When the peppers, onion and tomatoes are tender, take the saj tava from the fire. Bon apetit.
